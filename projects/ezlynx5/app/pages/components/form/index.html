Form Elements

Labels and Form Elements

  • Labels should always be displayed to the left of the form element.
  • When laying out forms in columns like the example below, layout the forms top to bottom first, and then left to right
  • Don't use more than three columns of form elements

 

Form Heading

 


<div class="row-fluid">
    <div class="span1">
        <label>Label 1</label>
    </div>
    <div class="span3">
        <select class="valid">
            <option value=""><select></option>
        </select>
    </div>
    <div class="span1">
        <label>Label 2</label>
    </div>
    <div class="span3">
        <input id="" maxlength="100" name="" type="text" value="" class="valid">
    </div>
    <div class="span1">
        <label for="">Label 3</label>
    </div>
    <div class="span3">
        <input id="" maxlength="50" name="" type="text" value="">
    </div>
</div>

 

Checkbox with label

Label should be to the right of the checkbox and have the label in Title Case.

 


<label> 
    <input id="" type="checkbox"> Test Me 
</label>

 

Radio button with label

Label should be to the right of the radio button and have the label in Title Case.

 


<label> 
    <input id="" type="radio"> Test Me 
</label>
