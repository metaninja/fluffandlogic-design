Icons

Use icons to illustrate actions or in place of words when space is limited e.g. to represent a validation error or warning.

There are a lot of icons to choose from. Use Ctrl + F in your browser to search for icons by their name. Most of the names should be pretty familiar e.g. time or star.

When displaying icons, use the following format. Add the icon-large class to display larger icons.


<i class="icon-warning-sign"></i> Warning: You will lose all your changes.
<i class="icon-info icon-large"></i> Note: This is a large icon.
